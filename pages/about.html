about me

I am a Software Engineer and a Videogame Programmer.

My special interest is in designing and programming levels and mechanics to make interactive games and that players feel comfortable with the proposal.

I studied three years at ESAT, Valencia, where I learned the basics of video game programming in C and C++, as well as database management and the creation and design of web pages. The modularization of code using the paradigm of object-oriented programming, teamwork and proper organization of work and time, were some of the qualities I learned during my stay at ESAT.

I have worked with C++ development libraries such as SDL2 and Allegro5, and some industry standards such as Unity Engine and Unreal Engine, creating small prototypes of video games implementing different game mechanics.

I am a dynamic person, easy to adapt to changes, and willing to propose and help to bring creative ideas forward.
